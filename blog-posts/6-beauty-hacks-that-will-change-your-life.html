6 Beauty Hacks That Will Change Your Life

Beauty Hack #1
Using an eyebrow pen as a matte lipstick

This hack is simple; you will need to moisturize your lips and lightly go over your lips with an eyebrow pen starting with your bottom lip and finishing up with your upper lip. Once you are done applying eyebrow pen, place a napkin between your lips and bite a napkin with your lips to eliminate the excess product off your lips, this last step will achieve the matte look. This hack works best with deep colors. Remember, while doing this process do not press your lips together as this will make your product build up and be clumpy, only press your lips on the napkin step.

Beauty Hack #2
Iced water to tighten pores in your face

Cold water tightens up your skin pores and keeps pour skin looking healthy and flawless. Before starting off your makeup routine, you may like to add this pre-makeup routine. Fill up a bowl of ice water and soak your face in for 20 seconds before towel drying it up and apply your primer and makeup. Your make up will appear flawless and will last longer on your face.

Beauty Hack #3
No more dandruff and itches with Baking Soda Vinegar shampoo

Dandruff and itch are one of the embarrassing things that can happen with your hair. To get rid of dandruff, oil buildup and itch, you will need to mix 2tps of apple cider vinegar and 1tps of baking soda in a cup of water and stir to solute. Apply the product in your hair and let it sit for at least 3 minutes before rinsing it thoroughly. You may make your special shampoo in this way, to remove the buildup caused by the chemicals in your everyday shampoo product. This trick works like a charm, and it will save you money. However be careful not to use this “special shampoo” daily as it may damage your hair and dry your scalp.

Beauty Hack #4
Prime your eyelashes with castor oil to get rid of clumpy mascara lashes

We all love to have that bold, beautiful looking lashes, so sometimes we may be dipping in a little too much in that mascara jar to achieve that bold, thick seductive eyelashes. However, by doing so, we end up with clumpy looking lases. This trick makes your lashes appear longer and silky. With your old mascara brush carefully apply castor oil in your lashes before applying mascara. This trick gets rid of clumpy lashes that are caused by excessive mascara. Castor oil also fight hair loss and thinning hair, so it best to keep one handy as it is proved to be useful for many things including skin, hair and nails

Beauty Hack #5
Tight-line eyelash enhancer

One of my favorite eyelash hacks is mixing lysine eye drop with matter brown/black eyeshadow to enhance lashes appearance. Once done mixing the product, start by working from the inner corner of your lash line and working outwards by drawing an overlapping dotted line with your mixture using your thin brush. The finished product should be a tucked line at the base of your eyelash and be careful not to get it in the inner membrane and also for this to look presentable don’t get the eyelid.

Beauty Hack #6
Lipstick as a blush

As a dark skin girl, most times my skin absorbs a light blush and if it does appear. It just appears ashy, so instead of using a light blush to achieve rosy cheeks I use a rose lipstick and top it with shimmers. The good part about this hack is that it works for all skin types and is not limited to dark skin tones only. So whenever you find yourself at a party and needing to freshen but didn’t bring your brush, rub a small amount of your favorite lipstick on your cheeks and even if it the same lipstick you are wearing on your lips, nobody is going to notice the difference. Side note; eyeshadow makes a good blush as well, so if you have run out of lipstick, use the eyeshadow as a blush.
